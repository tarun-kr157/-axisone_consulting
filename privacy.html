Privacy Policy

At AxisOne Consulting, your privacy is a cornerstone of our professional relationship. We are committed to protecting the integrity and confidentiality of your data.

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Information We Collect

AxisOne Consulting collects information to provide better services to our clients and to improve our digital solutions. This collection occurs through our website, consulting engagements, and professional communications.

Personal Information

  • Contact details (Name, business email, phone number)
  • Professional background and organizational information
  • Technical data provided during consulting assessments
  • Correspondence and feedback regarding our services

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website. This includes your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the duration of your visit to ensure our platform remains secure and optimized for our users.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for professional purposes to deliver, maintain, and protect our consulting services.

Primary Uses

  1. To provide tailored strategic consulting and digital solutions
  2. To facilitate communication regarding project updates and milestones
  3. To process service agreements and professional contracts
  4. To comply with legal obligations and industry regulations
  5. To improve our website functionality and user experience

Information Sharing and Disclosure

AxisOne Consulting does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Disclosure only occurs when necessary for service delivery (e.g., trusted technical partners) or when required by law.

Confidentiality Guarantee

As a strategic firm, we adhere to strict non-disclosure protocols. Your proprietary business data and personal information are handled with the highest level of professional discretion.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your personal data.

Advanced Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted during transit and at rest using modern cryptographic protocols.

Access Control

Strict internal access controls ensure that only authorized personnel can handle your data.

Client Rights

You have specific rights regarding the personal information we hold about you. AxisOne Consulting provides full transparency in how you can manage this data.

Right to Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.

Right to Rectification

You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure

You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies to enhance navigation and analyze traffic patterns to better serve our digital audience.
